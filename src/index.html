JACOB SCHATZ

#1 Handsome Man

Jacob started programming in 2006. He hasn't stopped since. He is always writing code and coming up with crazy ideas. He spends most of his time writing Javascript, HTML and CSS. He also enjoys writing many other languages. He has written 2 books on Swift for Pearson. He has a popular YouTube channel under a secret pseudonym. He has a degree in piano performance and plays classical concerts frequently. Overall a very nice guy.

Jacob started at GitLab in 2015 as the first frontend engineer. He went on to lead the frontend team, UX team, and lead an awesome data science GitLab startup: Meltano. He has touched almost all parts of GitLab in his time there. He came up with the idea for the WebIDE and created the first prototype among other things.

Jacob Schatz works at Netlify as the Product Engineering Manager.

Jacob also speaks at conferences. He has spoken all over the world about VueJS.

Jacob also loves working on electronics and creates really fun electric vehicles. His goal in life is to use gas cars less and use electric vehicles more.

Jacob is a proud member of Little People of America. Although he does not have dwarfism himself, he has 2 sons who both have a very rare form of dwarfism that affects 1 in 500,000 people in the world.
